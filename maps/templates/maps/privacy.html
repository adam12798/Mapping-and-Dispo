Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Sutton by Iceberg Home Solutions ("we", "us", "our") operates the Sutton scheduling and appointment management platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information in the course of providing our scheduling and appointment management services:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, address)
  • Appointment details (date, time, type of service)
  • Communication records (SMS messages, voice call transcripts)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Send appointment reminders and updates via SMS
  • Coordinate sales representative schedules
  • Notify team members of schedule changes, time off requests, and available appointments

SMS Messaging and Consent

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS consent with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opt-In: SMS consent is obtained through our internal platform during employee onboarding. When a manager adds a sales representative to the Sutton platform, the representative's phone number is entered and the "SMS Consent" checkbox is checked to confirm the representative has agreed to receive work-related SMS messages. Upon opt-in, the representative receives a confirmation SMS with opt-out instructions before any other messages are sent. Representatives may also opt in by texting START to our messaging number. For full details, see our SMS Consent & Opt-In page.

Types of Messages: Appointment scheduling alerts, appointment reminders, disposition follow-up reminders, time off request notifications, and team coordination messages.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on appointment activity, typically 1-10 messages per week.

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also reply CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, or REVOKE. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and will receive no further messages. You may re-subscribe at any time by texting START.

Help: For help, reply HELP or INFO to any message, or call us at (833) 799-0424.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — such as Twilio (SMS/voice), solely to deliver our services
  • As required by law — to comply with legal obligations

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
