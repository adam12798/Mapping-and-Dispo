SMS Consent & Opt-In

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Sutton by Iceberg Home Solutions uses SMS messaging to communicate with our employed sales representatives for appointment scheduling, team coordination, and operational updates.

Who Receives Messages

Only sales representatives who are onboarded into the Sutton platform receive SMS messages. Messages are sent exclusively to employees of Iceberg Home Solutions and its affiliated brands — we do not send marketing messages to consumers or the general public.

How Representatives Opt In

SMS consent is obtained through our internal platform during the employee onboarding process:

  1. A manager adds the sales representative to the Sutton platform and enters their phone number
  2. The manager checks the "SMS Consent" box, confirming the rep has agreed to receive work-related SMS messages
  3. The representative receives a confirmation SMS with opt-out instructions before any other messages are sent

SMS Consent (as shown in our platform):

Representatives may also opt in by texting START to our messaging number.

Opt-In Confirmation Message

Upon opting in, each representative receives the following confirmation message:

Sutton by Iceberg Home Solutions: You're now subscribed to appointment updates. Msg frequency varies. Msg&Data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel.

Types of Messages

Representatives may receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Available appointment alerts (TextBlast)
  • Appointment reminders and schedule updates
  • Disposition follow-up reminders
  • Time off request approvals and notifications

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on appointment activity, typically 1-10 messages per week.

Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and will not receive any further messages. You may also text CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, or REVOKE.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at info@iceberghomesolutions.com.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in consent with any third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
